The Bunny

What kind of animal it is

The bunny, or rabbit, is a small mammal belonging to the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha.

Where it is located

Bunnies are found in many parts of the world, including Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas. They thrive in diverse habitats.

Biological features

  • Scientific Name: Oryctolagus cuniculus
  • Weight: 2-4 pounds
  • Length: 12-16 inches
  • Soft fur, long ears, and a short fluffy tail

Habitat

Bunnies typically live in burrows or warrens. They are often found in meadows, woods, forests, grasslands, deserts, and wetlands.
